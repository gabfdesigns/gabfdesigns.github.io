Reviews

“Amazing Brewing Co., as the name confidently state, is an exceptional brewery worth a visit for anyone serious about good beer and awesome food. They have incredible 40+ selections from their own ales to sought-after rare brews from other countries. What's more respectable is how they manage and keep every tap clean and fresh.”

— Roebelling, Trip Advisor

“This brewpub makes and sells beer from inside a renovated wooden building that was built in 1959. They have 59 tabs in total—five are for the beers brewed here and the rest are for the ones from other breweries. The Amazing Brewing Company opens at 12pm and they pay special attention to their food as well…What does all of this mean for those of us here in Korea? That it’s a great place to get some day drinking done.”

— Time Out

“Huge selection of beers. The sampler is where the fun is at: 8 different beers, 5 classics and 3 of our choosing, 100ml of each. It's a fun tasting experience and, if you feel like more, will make sure you can go for a big glass of your favorite.”

— Raphael, Mango Plate
